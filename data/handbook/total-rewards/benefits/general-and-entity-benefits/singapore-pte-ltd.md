## We've Got You Covered

At GitLab, we believe team member benefits should be simple to understand and there when you need them. From life's big moments to everyday questions, our comprehensive coverage gives you clarity and confidence so you can focus on your work and life.
In addition to the local benefits listed below, all Singapore team members have access to the [company-wide benefits](/handbook/total-rewards/benefits/general-and-entity-benefits/) available to all team members globally.

## Financial Security

### Life Insurance

GitLab provides company-paid Group Term Life Insurance through HSBC Insurance with coverage of SGD 500,000 per team member.

- **Automatic coverage**: SGD 150,000 (no medical questions required)
- **Full coverage**: SGD 500,000 (requires completing medical underwriting)
- See slides 37-42 of the [GitLab Singapore Benefit Overview](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1EhdoC9Dgkf_5919-WZeuBJtdWeNz8hWp/view?usp=sharing) for complete details

Singapore's Central Provident Fund (CPF) provides comprehensive retirement security. While GitLab doesn't offer supplementary private pension benefits, the CPF system ensures your long-term financial wellbeing. Learn more at [cpf.gov.sg](http://cpf.gov.sg).

## Growing Family Leaves

GitLab Singapore combines statutory leave with our global parental leave policy to provide comprehensive support for growing families.

### Maternity Leave

Team members employed for at least 3 months before birth may be eligible for the maternity leaves listed below.

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Government-Paid Maternity Leave (16 weeks)</summary>

- **1st and 2nd births**: 8 weeks employer-paid + 8 weeks government-paid
- **3rd+ births**: 16 weeks government-paid
- First 8 weeks must be continuous; remaining weeks flexible within 12 months of birth
- Eligible team members receive concurrent [GitLab Parental Leave](/handbook/total-rewards/benefits/general-and-entity-benefits/#parental-leave)

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Employment Act Maternity Leave (12 weeks)</summary>

- 8 weeks paid + 4 weeks unpaid
- With [GitLab Parental Leave](/handbook/total-rewards/benefits/general-and-entity-benefits/#parental-leave), eligible team members receive up to 16 weeks paid

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary>How to Apply</summary>

1. Submit "Parental Leave" request in Workday at least 30 days before leave
2. Send [GPML Declaration Form](https://file.go.gov.sg/gpml1form.pdf) to leaves@gitlab.com
3. GitLab handles government reimbursement claims

</details>

### Paternity Leave

Team members employed for at least 3 months before due date may receive:

- 4 weeks government-paid paternity leave
- Up to 16 weeks total when combined with [GitLab Parental Leave](/handbook/total-rewards/benefits/general-and-entity-benefits/#parental-leave)
 for eligible team members

<details markdown="1">
<summary>How to Apply</summary>

1. Submit "Parental Leave" request in Workday
2. Send [GPPL Declaration Form](https://file.go.gov.sg/gpfd1.pdf) to leaves@gitlab.com

Learn more about eligibility at the [Ministry of Manpower website](https://www.mom.gov.sg/employment-practices/leave/paternity-leave).

</details>

### Shared Parental Leave

Team members whose child's date of birth, Estimated Delivery Date (EDD), or Formal Intent to Adopt Date (FIAD) is on or after 1 April 2026 are eligible for **10 weeks** of government-paid SPL, in addition to the existing Maternity and Paternity Leave entitlements above.

- 10 weeks shared between both parents (5 weeks each by default; reallocation available via the LifeSG portal)
- Fully government-paid, capped at SGD 2,500 per week including CPF contributions
- Must be used within 12 months of the child's birth
- Learn more about eligibility at the [Ministry of Manpower website](https://www.mom.gov.sg/employment-practices/leave/shared-parental-leave).

<details markdown="1">
<summary>How to Apply</summary>

1. Provide at least 4 weeks' notice before commencing a continuous block of SPL
2. Request "Shared Parental Leave" by emailing leaves@gitlab.com

</details>

## Time off Policies

### Annual Leave

- Holiday allowance: Holiday allowance: After 3 months of employment, accrual rates will be based on tenure. Team members are allowed to carry over unused vacation up to the statutory accrual limit based on tenure.
  - 1 year of service: 7 days/year
  - 2 years of service: 8 days/year
  - 3 years of service: 9 days/year
  - 4 years of service: 10 days/year
  - 5 years of service: 11 days/year
  - 6 years of service: 12 days/year
  - 7 years of service: 13 days/year
  - 8 years of service: 14 days/year
  
- Runs concurrently with GitLab PTO
- Enter time off in Workday using "Paid Time Off" option
- Coordinate with your team to ensure coverage
- Unused leave requires approval to carry forward

### Other Leave Entitlements

As provided under Singapore law (Child Development Co-Savings Act and the Employment Act):

- Outpatient sick leave
- Hospitalization leave
- Adoption leave
- Childcare and extended childcare leave
- Unpaid infant care leave
