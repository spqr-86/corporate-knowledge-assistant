## Healthcare Benefits

Monthly healthcare allowance of €70 providing flexibility to choose your own healthcare plan.

## Financial Security

### Mandatory Indexation

Wages automatically adjust by 2% to maintain purchasing power under Joint Industrial Committee 227 (JIC 227).

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Indexation Details</summary>

Belgian team members fall under Joint Industrial Committee 227 (JIC 227). Wages automatically adjust by 2% when the smoothed health index reaches pivot numbers to maintain purchasing power. May be processed as part of Annual Compensation Review.
</details>

### Ecocheques

Annual electronic vouchers up to €250 for ecological products and services.

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Ecocheques Details</summary>

**Key Features**

- Maximum €250 annually (prorated by start date)
- Delivered via Sodexo card by July 31st
- Reference period: July 1 - June 30
- Exempt from social security contributions

**Approved Uses**
Review eligible products on the [National Labor Council website](https://cnt-nar.be/sites/default/files/documents/nl/cao-098-quinquies-%2823-05-2017%29.pdf)
</details>

### Meal Vouchers

Effective 2026-03-01, all Belgium-based team members are entitled to meal vouchers. This tax-advantaged benefit helps cover daily meal expenses and can be used at restaurants, food stores, and food delivery services throughout Belgium.

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Eligibility Requirements</summary>

- All GitLab Belgium Team Members
- Prorated based on actual worked days (excludes weekends, public holidays, vacation days, sick leave and unpaid leave)
- Team members receive meal vouchers based on their worked days during the previous month for the current month. E.g., if you worked 19 days in February, 19 meal vouchers (nominal value: €8) will be loaded to your meal card in March
- Part-time team members receive vouchers proportional to their working schedule
- Team members should generally expect their Pluxee meal cards to be loaded with meal vouchers for the previous month by the **third week of each month**, following payroll submission by our local payroll provider.

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Coverage & Key Features</summary>

- Daily Value: €8.00 per worked day
- Employer Contribution: €6.91 (tax-free)
- Team Member Contribution: €1.09 (deducted from net salary)
- Provider: Pluxee
- Format: Electronic Meal Card
- Validity: Meal vouchers are valid for 12 months from the date of issuance

</details>

### Sick Leave

30 days full salary continuation, runs concurrently with GitLab's global Sick Time policy.

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Sick Leave Details</summary>

- 30 days full salary continuation (regardless of seniority)
- Runs concurrently with GitLab's global Sick Time policy
- Designate as "Out Sick" in Workday
- Consecutive illnesses within 14 days count as one period unless medical certificate proves new illness

</details>

### Parental Leave

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Maternity Leave Details</summary>

**Maternity Leave**

- 15 weeks total (6 prenatal, 9 postnatal)
- For team members with 6+ months tenure: 100% salary for up to 16 weeks
- Runs concurrently with [GitLab Parental Leave](/handbook/total-rewards/benefits/general-and-entity-benefits/#parental-leave)
- Request via Workday 3 months before start date

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Paternity/Co-Parent Leave Details</summary>

**Paternity/Co-Parent Leave**

- 20 days leave (15 days for births before January 1, 2023)
- Use within 4 months of birth
- 100% salary for up to 16 weeks (minus statutory entitlements)
- Runs concurrently with [GitLab's 16-week Parental Leave policy](/handbook/total-rewards/benefits/general-and-entity-benefits/#parental-leave)
- GitLab tops up mutual insurance fund payments to 100% salary

</details>

### Vacation Leave

20 statutory vacation days annually, runs concurrently with GitLab PTO.

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Vacation Leave Details</summary>

- 20 statutory days, front-loaded annually for full-time team members and does not carry over to the next calendar year
- Vacation certificate provided for job transitions
- Runs concurrently with GitLab PTO

</details>

### Circumstantial Leave (Klein Verlet)

Paid leave for personal matters per [klein verlet](https://werk.belgie.be/nl/themas/arbeidsovereenkomsten/schorsing-van-de-arbeidsovereenkomst/oorzaken-van-schorsing-hoofde-12) regulations.

<details markdown="1">
<summary>How to Request Circumstantial Leave</summary>

**How to Request**

1. Navigate to "Request Absence" in Workday
2. Select "Belgium Circumstantial" under Leaves
3. Choose appropriate reason and submit

</details>
